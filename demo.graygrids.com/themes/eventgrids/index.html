We create and turn into reality

NIPUNA, the National Level Techno-Management Symposium organized by the ISTE student chapter at SRKREC for nearly three decades, is a hub of excellence and innovation. This annual event provides a dynamic platform for students to showcase their talents through workshops, seminars, and technical and non-technical competitions. Beyond the classroom, participants gain hands-on experience, refine problem-solving skills, and engage in friendly competition. As a visionary knowledge nexus, NIPUNA fosters creativity and knowledge exchange, shaping future leaders and innovators poised to make a lasting impact.
